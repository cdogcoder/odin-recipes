Cupcakes

Description

Craving a nice treat on a nice spring day? Try out our famous cupcake recipe!

Amazingly soft and moist in the middle, and customizable in every regard, these cupcakes are out-of-this-world creations that you must make for friends and family, or if no one's watching, just for yourself! Give it a try today, and come back soon!

Ingredients

  • Baking mix
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Sprinkles
  • Frosting
  • Love

Steps

  1. Combine all wet ingredients until you get thick batter consistency
  2. Load batter into paper holders in cupcake tray
  3. Put the cupcake tray in the oven at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 15 minutes; Once out of the oven, let it rest for minutes
  4. Once rested, pop the cupcakes out of the molds and decorate to your liking using frosting and sprinkles
  5. Once decorated, plate the cupcakes and serve warm

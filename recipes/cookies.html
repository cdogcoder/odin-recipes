Cookies

Description

Can't get enough of freshly baked cookies? Try out our famous cookie recipe!

Freshly baked and steaming hot when they come out of the oven, these chocolate chip cookies are perfect for any occasion, whether you share them with friends and family... or snack on all of them yourself. Give it a try today, you won't regret it!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate chips
  • Love

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs in with the flour until it becomes a dough
  2. Once slightly doughy, mix in chocolate chips to your liking
  3. Knead until dough forms and leave in frdige to set for 10-15 minutes
  4. Once out of fridge, roll out dough and cut circle shapes
  5. Put the cookies on a rack and bake them at 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 10 minutes. Once they're out, serve warm.
